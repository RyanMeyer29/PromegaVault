#Definitions

LYO Finishing B (room 1275, ISO CNC) is a room in the [[Buildings/Feynman\|Feynman]] building where vials go after being filled and sealed in the lyophilizer. This is where labeling and packaging of finished vials takes place. It is the room commonly referred to as the "finishing station" within fill/finish operations. The room is 2,811 SF and is located on the first floor of Feynman adjacent to LYO Finishing A and LYO Production.
