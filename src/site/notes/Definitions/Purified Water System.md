#Definitions

A Purified Water System increases the quality of municipal water supply for use in manufacturing processes. At Promega, purified water is typically produced using reverse osmosis and electric de-ionization to meet USP water standards. Not all facilities have purified water systems — only those requiring higher water quality for their processes.
