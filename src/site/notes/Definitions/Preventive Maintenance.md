#Definitions

Preventive Maintenance (PM) is a scheduled maintenance process applied to production equipment at Promega. PMs are carried out on a regular basis to keep equipment in good working condition and prevent unplanned downtime. Because multiple systems are interdependent on the production floor, a PM on one piece of equipment can have downstream effects on others — for example, a KPA air pressure loss was noted as likely fallout from a recent PM.
