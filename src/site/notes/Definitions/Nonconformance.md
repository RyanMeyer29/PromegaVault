#Definitions

A Nonconformance (NC) is a documented record used when a product, process, or event deviates from expected standards or specifications. When a nonconformance occurs or is detected, a nonconformance record is used to document the event, its cause, actions taken, and to determine whether to escalate the issue. Nonconformances are governed at Promega by QP22 alongside the [[Definitions/CAPA\|CAPA]] process.
