#Definitions

Spectrum refers to the [[Definitions/Promega\|Promega]] Spectrum Capillary [[Definitions/Electrophoresis\|Electrophoresis]] device, used to analyze DNA. The most common customer use cases for this device are forensics and paternity testing, to match one set of DNA to another.
