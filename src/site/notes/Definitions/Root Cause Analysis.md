#Definitions

Root Cause Analysis (RCA) is a structured process for investigating the underlying cause of an equipment failure, quality issue, or nonconformance. Rather than addressing symptoms alone, RCA aims to identify and resolve the fundamental reason a problem occurred so it does not recur. It is commonly associated with quality and corrective action processes at Promega.
