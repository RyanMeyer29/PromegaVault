#Definitions

Packaging Engineering is a department at Promega based in [[Buildings/Lamarr\|Lamarr]], focused on packaging development and engineering. The team includes packaging engineers. As of early 2026, the supervisor is [[People/Kevin Rogers\|Kevin Rogers]], who reports to [[People/Andy Swetlik\|Andy Swetlik]].
