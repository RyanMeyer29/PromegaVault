#Definitions

CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Action) is a quality process at Promega involving in-depth investigation of product, process, and quality system issues. It draws on quality data from sources including nonconformances and complaints, and tracks actions through to completion with effectiveness assessments. A cross-functional CAPA Board made up of managers and directors from Development, Manufacturing/Operations, and QA oversees the process. It is governed by QP22 alongside [[Definitions/Nonconformance\|Nonconformances]].
