#Definitions

A Building Automation System (BAS) is a network of interconnected systems designed to manage and control various functions in a building, including HVAC, lighting, and security. At Promega, the Facilities department uses the BAS to track, trend, and alarm HVAC, safety systems, and process equipment across campus buildings. It is governed by QSOP13.003.
