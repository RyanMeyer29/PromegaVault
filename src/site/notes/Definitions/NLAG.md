#Definitions

NLAG is an SAP material type for non-stocked, non-valuated maintenance supplies. This typically covers shop and production supplies — things like gloves, bags, or other consumables that are ordered regularly but not tracked as inventory in SAP. Creating a material as an NLAG in SAP allows purchase orders for it to auto-populate many required fields, rather than requiring manual entry each time.
