#Definitions

Metrology is a department at Promega responsible for the calibration of measurement equipment used across the company. The department is located in [[Buildings/Feynman\|Feynman]] and maintains calibration standards for a range of equipment types including temperature probes, balances, and pressure instruments. [[People/Kimberly Steinhauer\|Kimberly Steinhauer]] is the Supervisor of Metrology. I first encountered this department through the [[My Projects/Calibration Probe Fixture\|Calibration Probe Fixture]] project.
