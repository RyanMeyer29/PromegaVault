#Definitions

A Lightnin Mixer is an industrial mixing device used in bulk production operations at Promega. They are used in [[Definitions/In Vitro Dispensing\|IVD]] operations and are present in multiple buildings including [[Buildings/Feynman\|Feynman]] and [[Buildings/BTC\|BTC]]. The [[My Projects/Mixer Chuck\|Mixer Chuck]] project involves work related to these mixers.
