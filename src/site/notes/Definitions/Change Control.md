#Definitions

Change Control (CC) is a formal process at Promega for identifying, evaluating, approving, and implementing changes. It covers a wide scope including changes to facilities, utilities, equipment, products, processes, software, and documents. It is governed by QP24 (Rev 1). Change Control is a key step in deploying new or modified production parts and equipment, and is something I encounter in fill/finish engineering work.
