#Definitions

Septa Mats are gray rubber (?) mats that have a number of protruding wells on them, resembling Phillips head screwdrivers. The number of wells depends on if it is meant for a 72 well plate or a [[Definitions/96 Well Plate\|96 Well Plate]]. They lay over top of the plate as it is used in the [[Definitions/Spectrum\|Spectrum]] Capillary [[Definitions/Electrophoresis\|Electrophoresis]] device
