#Definitions

A cleanroom is a controlled environment where airborne particulates, temperature, and humidity are tightly regulated to prevent contamination of products or processes. In the [[Buildings/Feynman\|Feynman]] building, cleanrooms are used for fill/finish operations on the MA lines. The terms cleanroom and [[Definitions/ECR\|ECR]] (Environmentally Controlled Room) are often used interchangeably at Promega, though they may be technically distinct.
