Optima is an equipment manufacturer whose name is used to identify the bottling sub-area of FAB, known as FAB Optima. Optima has a Germany branch and has supplied equipment to Promega including weigh cells on MA2.

#Definitions

> [!info]- Details & Notes
>
> **Unknowns:** Weigh cells supplied on MA2 are noted as obsolete and may need replacement with a different model.
