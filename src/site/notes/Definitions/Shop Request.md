#Definitions

A shop request is the standard form used to request machined parts be fabricated at Promega, whether internally or through an external vendor. Before submitting, drawings and models must be checked into ePDM and SAP part numbers must be assigned. The same form applies regardless of whether parts are made in-house or sent out.
