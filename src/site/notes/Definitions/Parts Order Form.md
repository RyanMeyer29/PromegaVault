#Definitions

The Parts Order Form is a Smartsheet-based form used to request new SAP part numbers for individual parts. It is the day-to-day tool for submitting part number requests when ordering fewer than 5 materials — more intuitive than the Excel-based Multiple Materials Request Spreadsheet, which is reserved for bulk requests of 5 or more. As the form is filled out, roadblocks or open questions are noted and tracked. The primary contact for part number requests is Nikki Masterson.
