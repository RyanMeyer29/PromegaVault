#Definitions

ESST is a vendor whose name is used to identify the non-cleanroom sub-area of FAB, known as FAB ESST. The full vendor identity is not confirmed. In SAP, the FAB ESST functional location (equipment number 1012, location MAD-GMP-1227) is described as Bottle Seal & Label — which reflects the primary activity of the line as a whole. Known issues in this area include ink/grease marks on bottles and wrinkled labels.
