The Maxwell® RSC is an automated nucleic acid extraction instrument made by Promega. It uses cartridges filled and labeled on the MaxAuto lines. One known cartridge type is the AS140A, which is used in the Maxwell® RSC Plant DNA Kit for extracting genomic DNA from plant tissue samples.
