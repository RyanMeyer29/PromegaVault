#Fabrication

The Markforged Industrial X7 is a high-performance industrial composite 3D printer. It prints in Onyx and can continuously reinforce parts with carbon fiber, fiberglass, or Kevlar, producing parts with significantly higher strength than standard FDM printers. It is the larger industrial variant of the Markforged lineup.
