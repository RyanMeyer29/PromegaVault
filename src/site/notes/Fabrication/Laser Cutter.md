#Fabrication

The laser cutter is a fabrication tool at [[Buildings/Lamarr\|Lamarr]] used for cutting thin or detailed parts from sheet material. It is particularly useful for thin plastics that are difficult to cut cleanly on the [[Fabrication/Zund\|Zund]]. I used it for cutting comb parts for the [[My Projects/Gel Electrophoresis Tray\|Gel Electrophoresis Tray]] project.
