## Changing a routing bit
From Main menu:
1. Go to Module 3 page (F3)
2. Select Tool (1)
3. Click OK
4. A prompt will warn you that the cutter is moving
5. Click OK
6. Wait for cutter to come to corner
7. Unscrew black knob
8. Take the assembly out
9. Click OK to open collet
10. Change tool, pull it out about 1/4 in to allow clearance for collet closing
11. Click OK to close collet
12. Place assembly back on to the arm, and make sure it is rotated to slot into position
13. Tighten the knob
14. Click OK
15. Auto Initialize from Module 3 menu 
	1. Click Initialization (2)
	2. Auto-init (2)
	3. Click Start
