#Software

Engineering Workstations (EWS) are remote desktop servers used by engineering staff to access fermentation control software and the [[Software/Batch\|Batch]] system. They are separate from the operator [[Definitions/HMI\|HMI]] terminals. Access is through a virtual desktop environment.
