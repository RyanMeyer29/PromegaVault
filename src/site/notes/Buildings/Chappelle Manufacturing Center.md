#Buildings

The Chappelle Manufacturing Center (CMC) is a Promega building that produces the small molecule components used in Promega manufacturing processes that regulate biological processes. Staff include organic chemists, engineers, environmental health and safety professionals, quality control chemists, and maintenance and logistics staff. The CMC is approximately 160,000 sq ft (14,864 sq meters) and complements the existing manufacturing capacities in Madison and California. It is located at 3075 Sub Zero Parkway, Madison, WI 53719. A large oil bath stored at CMC is referenced as a backup resource for the [[My Projects/Calibration Probe Fixture\|Calibration Probe Fixture]] project.
