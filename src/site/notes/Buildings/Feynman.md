#Buildings

Feynman is the building where the fill/finish team is based, including the Operations Engineering group. It contains multiple Environmentally Controlled Rooms (ECRs) which house the filling and sealing production lines — including [[Lines/MA1\|MA1]], [[Lines/MA2\|MA2]], [[Lines/MA3/MA3\|MA3]], [[Lines/FAB\|FAB]], and the [[Lines/KPA\|KPA]]. This is the building I work in day-to-day as part of the fill/finish team. Products filled here include [[Definitions/Polymerase Chain Reaction\|PCR]] reagents such as polymerases and mastermixes that are distributed to research labs worldwide. There is a lunch spot on-site that operates as a bowl station and soup café, offering specialty bowls that rotate monthly as well as a build-your-own bowl option where you choose your own ingredients.
