#Buildings

The Arnold Center is a Promega building that houses the fermentation operations, including the [[My Projects/ArC Notes/Fermenters\|fermenter]] vessels, [[My Projects/ArC Notes/Centrifuge\|centrifuges]], and associated control systems. This is where [[My Projects/ArC Notes/Fermentation\|fermentation]] runs are executed — from [[My Projects/ArC Notes/Fermentation Media\|media]] prep and sterilization through [[My Projects/ArC Notes/Biomass\|biomass]] growth, [[My Projects/ArC Notes/Induction\|induction]], and harvest. Cell paste and [[My Projects/ArC Notes/Supernatant\|supernatant]] produced here are either frozen on-site or shipped to downstream facilities like [[Buildings/RFC\|RFC]].

Internal Promega facilities documents also list it with the designation RDC.
