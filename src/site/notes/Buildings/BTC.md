#Buildings

BTC stands for BioPharmaceutical Technology Center. It houses at least two [[Definitions/Lyophilizer\|lyophilizers]] as well as a number of scientists, though the exact nature of their work is unclear — research scientists are primarily based in [[Buildings/Kornberg\|Kornberg]], so the scientists at BTC likely serve a different function. There is a café on-site that serves a variety of food including sandwiches, burgers, pizza, and a salad bar. The café features a pizza of the month and a burger of the month.
