Feynman Automatic Bottling (FAB) is a bottling line located in the Feynman building. It is divided into two sub-areas: FAB ESST, which is the non-clean-room area, and FAB Optima, which is the bottling area. The names come from the companies that manufactured the equipment in each area. [[People/Stirling Mitchell\|Stirling Mitchell]] handles work here. Known activities include servo drive replacement and 180 mL seal testing.
