MA2 (MaxAuto2) is one of the MaxAuto fill/finish lines in the Feynman building. Like the other MaxAuto lines, it fills, seals, and labels cartridges used in Promega's Maxwell® RSC instrument kits.

#Lines

**Known Equipment:**

- Mixer
- Weigh cells _(Optima brand — obsolete, no longer in production. May require replacement with a different model. [[Definitions/Optima\|Optima]] Germany branch sourcing options)_
- Vacuum pump _(known issue: never shuts off; fix was implemented on MA2 and is being ported to MA3)_
- [[Definitions/HMI\|HMI]] units
