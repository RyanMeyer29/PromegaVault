The MaxAuto lines are a family of automated fill/finish production lines located in the Feynman building. They fill, seal, and label cartridges used in Promega's Maxwell® RSC instrument kits. These cartridges are shipped to customers around the world as components of finished kits. The lines are designated MA1, MA2, and MA3. The MaxAuto lines are part of the broader IVD Product Finishing operation, overseen by the Product Finishing Team.
