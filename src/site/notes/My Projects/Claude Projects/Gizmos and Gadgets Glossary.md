#My-Projects

The Gizmos and Gadgets Glossary is a shared knowledge base for Promega co-ops. It is designed to make common terms, acronyms, people, teams, and manufacturing lines easily accessible to new hires who are trying to get up to speed. The glossary lives as an Obsidian vault in Ryan's OneDrive, separate from his personal vault, and currently has roughly 10–30 entries. The long-term vision is to make it publicly accessible to all co-ops, though the delivery mechanism has not been decided yet.

The glossary is populated using two Claude skills working in sequence: the Brain Dump Interviewer collects raw knowledge from co-ops through a conversational interview and outputs a structured .txt file, and the New Hire Glossary Builder parses those .txt files and converts them into individual Obsidian-ready markdown notes. Brain dump data has been sent out to the other co-ops and is still being collected.

## Current State

- Vault exists on Ryan's OneDrive in a separate folder from his personal vault
- Roughly 10–30 entries populated so far
- Waiting on brain dump submissions from other co-ops

## Future Goals

- Collect brain dump submissions from all co-ops and process them into the vault
- Make the glossary accessible to all co-ops once it has enough content — delivery method TBD
- Tie into the New Hire Help Hub as a key resource for incoming co-ops
