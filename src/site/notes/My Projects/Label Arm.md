#My-Projects

[[People/Paul Doll\|Paul D]] needed a fix to an issue they have had in their vial labeler. It would shoot out vials, so an arm was created to hold them in place. This arm would sometimes cause the vials to lean at an angle and the labels would be applied askew. To fix this, I was tasked with modifying the arm to hold the vial on both sides in hopes to fix this issue. 


Created Dual Arm model.
